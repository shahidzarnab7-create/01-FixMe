Creamy White Sauce Pasta

A rich and creamy white sauce pasta recipe that's perfect for a satisfying dinner. This restaurant-style dish combines tender chicken, mushrooms, and penne pasta in a velvety cheese sauce that's simply irresistible.

Ingredients

1. Boneless chicken cubes 200g
2. Soy sauce 1 tbs
3. Pink salt ¼ tsp or to taste
4. Black pepper powder ¼ tsp
5. Dried oregano ¼ tsp
6. Cooking oil 1 tbs
7. Mushrooms sliced 1 Cup
8. Cooking oil 1 tsp
9. Unsalted Butter 2-3 tbs
10. Garlic chopped 1 tbs
11. All-purpose flour 2 tbs
12. Milk 2 Cups or as required
13. Cream 1/3 Cup
14. Cheddar cheese grated ½ Cup
15. Red chili crushed ½ tsp
16. Dried oregano ½ tsp
17. Pink salt ½ tsp or to taste
18. Black pepper crushed ½ tsp
19. Penne pasta 250g boiled
20. Fresh parsley chopped 2 tbs
21. Red chili crushed for garnish
22. Fresh parsley chopped for garnish

Directions

In a bowl, add chicken, soy sauce, pink salt, black pepper powder, dried oregano & mix well.
In a wok, add cooking oil, seasoned chicken, mix well & cook on medium flame for 4-5 minutes.
Add mushrooms, mix well & cook for 1-2 minutes & set aside.
In same wok, add cooking oil, margarine & let it melt.
Add garlic & sauté for a minute.
Add all-purpose flour, mix well & cook on low flame for 1-2 minutes.
Gradually add milk, mix well & cook until it thickens.
Turn off the flame, add cream, cheddar cheese, red crushed chili, dried oregano, pink salt, black pepper crushed, mix well, turn on the flame & cook for 1-2 minutes.
Add pasta, cooked chicken & mushrooms, fresh parsley & give it a good mix.
Sprinkle red chili crushed, fresh parsley & serve!
