Home Alone

Hey students! Get ready for an awesome Christmas adventure! This is the super funny story about Kevin, a kid who gets left home alone and has to be super brave to protect his house from silly burglars. Perfect for movie night with friends and family!

Visit Official Website

The Story

It's Christmas time and the McCallister family is getting ready for a big trip to Paris! But with so many people in the house, things get really crazy. When everyone wakes up late and rushes to the airport, nobody notices that 8-year-old Kevin is still at home!

At first, Kevin thinks it's awesome - no parents, no brothers and sisters telling him what to do! But then he discovers two goofy burglars called the "Wet Bandits" are trying to break into houses in his neighborhood. Kevin decides to be a hero and sets up lots of funny traps to stop them!

Using his clever ideas and everyday stuff from his house, Kevin creates amazing traps that keep the burglars away. It's full of laughs and excitement, and don't worry - Kevin's family comes back just in time for Christmas, and the bad guys get caught by the police!

Meet the Characters

Kevin McCallister

A super smart and brave 8-year-old who uses his creativity to protect his house. He shows that even kids can be heroes!

Harry Lyme

The leader of the burglars who thinks he's really smart but keeps getting caught in Kevin's funny traps.

Marv Merchants

Harry's goofy partner who's always making silly mistakes and getting hurt by the traps.

"Old Man" Marley

The mysterious neighbor who everyone thinks is scary, but he's actually really kind and helps Kevin.

Kate McCallister

Kevin's mom who loves him very much and does everything possible to get back home to him.

Buzz McCallister

Kevin's older brother who likes to tease him but actually cares about his little brother.
